How to view the Vehicle Identification Number (VIN)

All vehicles have a unique identification number, a VIN 1.

  1. Tap settings at the bottom of the center display.
  2. Proceed to System and then About.
    The VIN can also be found:
    • on the first page of the Warranty and Service Records Information booklet
    • n the vehicle's registration card
    • by looking at the dashboard through the vehicle's windshield.
