Glossary

Volvo ID

a personal ID that gives the access to a range of services using a single username and password. Volvo ID is needed is to check the vehicle via the phone using the Volvo Cars app.

Volvo cars app

an application that provides remote control functions to protect the vehicle and increase the comfort level.

ID

identification number.

All-wheel drive specific (AWD)

the drivetrain is powering all your wheels in order to have traction through better means. AWD provides improved traction on challenging road conditions, such as wet or snow-covered streets, ensuring a confident driving experience.
